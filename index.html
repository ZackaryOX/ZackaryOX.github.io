About Me

Programming is a second nature for me, it has always been a great passion of mine. I work best with a motivated group who all have equal stakes in the outcome of the project. While I enjoy programming in general my favorite is that which is geared towards video games. My strong suit is back end programming which involves system/framework development, I like to make tools and systems so other developer's lives are easier. While I enjoy back-end coding I have also done my fair share of front-end. I believe I am a jack of all trades but some things inspire me more than others. I am a highly motivated individual and when I start a project it is what I breathe, sleep and eat, some find a problem with that. I also enjoy team dynamics and communicating with fellow team members to ensure we are not only on the right track but also to build sort of a comradery. I am not great with overly artistic visuals, but what I lack in visuals I make up with in hard work, if you give me a chance you'll see just how true that is.
